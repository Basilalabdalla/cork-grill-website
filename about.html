Our Story

Welcome to Cork Grill

Located in the vibrant Victorian Quarter of Cork, Cork Grill has been serving up delicious, freshly prepared grilled food since [Year Established - e.g., 2015]. Our passion is simple: great food, made with quality ingredients, served with a smile.

From our signature kebabs, crafted with traditional recipes and the freshest accompaniments, to our juicy, handcrafted burgers and flavourful wraps, we aim to satisfy every craving. We believe in generous portions and providing excellent value for our customers. Whether you're grabbing a quick lunch, ordering dinner for the family, or seeking a satisfying late-night meal, Cork Grill is your go-to spot in Cork.

Our Mission

To be Cork's favourite destination for high-quality, delicious grilled food, offering a welcoming atmosphere and exceptional customer service for both dine-in and takeaway experiences. We strive to create memorable meals that bring people together.

Our Commitment

We are committed to using fresh, locally sourced ingredients where possible, maintaining the highest standards of hygiene, and continuously innovating our menu to bring exciting and authentic flavours to our cherished community.

Meet the Team (Concept)

Behind every great meal at Cork Grill is a dedicated and passionate team. While we can't introduce everyone individually here, know that our chefs bring years of experience and a love for grilling, our counter staff are always ready with a friendly welcome, and everyone involved is committed to making your Cork Grill experience a memorable one. We're a local business proud to be part of the Cork community.
