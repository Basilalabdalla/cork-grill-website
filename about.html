Our Story

Welcome to Cork Grill

Located in the vibrant Victorian Quarter of Cork, Cork Grill has been serving up delicious, freshly prepared grilled food since [Year Established - e.g., 2015]. Our passion is simple: great food, made with quality ingredients, served with a smile.

From our signature kebabs, crafted with traditional recipes and the freshest accompaniments, to our juicy, handcrafted burgers and flavourful wraps, we aim to satisfy every craving. We believe in generous portions and providing excellent value for our customers.

Our Mission

To be Cork's favourite destination for high-quality, delicious grilled food, offering a welcoming atmosphere and exceptional customer service for both dine-in and takeaway experiences.

Our Commitment

We are committed to using fresh, locally sourced ingredients where possible, maintaining high standards of hygiene, and continuously innovating our menu to bring exciting flavours to our community.
